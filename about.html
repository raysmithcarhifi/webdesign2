SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is how we make your new website found on the search engines by anyone searching for keywords and products or services that you provide. Google is the king of search engines and uses complicated algorithms and rules to sift and sort all the content on the web.

Our extensive knowledge can optimize your content and make it as search engine friendly as possible however we cannot guarantee you number one spot on Google and you should avoid anyone who says they can. If you have some unique selling point or some very exclusive product we can almost guarantee a success in being on the first page for a while. The search engines keep changing the rules but nothing beats good quality valid content and clean well written code, that is our specialty.
